I am a data scientist and researcher at Colorado School of Mines where I am completing my Master of Science in Geology (May 2021)

My graduate work focuses on quantifying the multi-scale vertical and lateral heterogenity of thin-bedded turbidites from sea-cliff outcrops in California. My undergraduate work was on soil trends from the Karoo Basin, South Africa and their relationship to Permian-Triassic climate change.

Most recently, I am working on a machine learning research project to identify mineralogy from fine-grained images of rock thin-sections. Through my graduate research, internship experience, and additional coursework, I have become very experienced with end-to-end data collection, analysis, modeling, visualization, and documentation.
