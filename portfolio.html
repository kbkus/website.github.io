Automated Mineralogy (Spring 2021)

GitHub Repository

In-progress with Dr. Zane Jobe, Colorado School of Mines


Geologists cut rocks into 0.03 mm thick slices in order to determine their composition (mineralogy). This process is crucial in order to understand the origin/formation of the rock, and can also help to identify subtle differences between visually similar rock samples.

Example of thin sections (http://www.microscopy-uk.org.uk/mag/artfeb04/iwouslides2.html)
